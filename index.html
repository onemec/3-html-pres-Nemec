Social Security

What seems like free money comes at a high cost...

The Goal

Social Security was originally created to help people budget money into a government fund which can be taken from when retired, handicapped, etc.

What is wrong with that?

To get your money, you need a good future workforce. I wouldn't bet my money on that.

The Economy Doesn't Help Much

Soon enough the stock market will fail again and your money will be forced to fall with it.

Unemployment

Even if we had a skilled workforce, unemployment can lead to a reduced social security budget.

Reduced Workforce

Continued technological advances and cheap foreign labour overtake local jobs and take money out of our economy.

Gambling

The whole premise of social security is gambling your money in a ponzi scheme. The government forces you to buy into this epedemic without any choice.
